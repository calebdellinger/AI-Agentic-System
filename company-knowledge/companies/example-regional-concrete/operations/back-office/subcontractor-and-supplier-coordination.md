# Subcontractor & supplier coordination (example)

**Intent:** As the company scales, dependencies (steel, inserts, pumps, testing) need a **default coordination pattern**.

## Touchpoints

- Single coordinator per job + backup named in writing.
- “Ready to receive work” criteria for each trade handoff (dimensions, tolerances, access).

## Failure modes to plan for

- Late survey / embed conflict → who stops pour authority.
- Backup supplier activation triggers (quality, availability).

*Sample only; tune for your contracts and region.*
