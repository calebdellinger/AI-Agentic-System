# Bid-to-cash handoff outline (example)

**Goal:** As workload grows, handoffs between **estimating**, **project setup**, and **finance** stay traceable.

## Estimating → operations setup

- Single system of record for **scope**, **alternates**, and **excluded** items.
- Known risks and qualifiers copied forward (weather, access, inspection lead time).

## Operations → billing

- Cost codes / SOV line mapping agreed before first pay app cycle.
- Change events: intake format + turnaround expectation (hours/days).

## Cross-cutting

- Archive path for RFIs / ASIs that affect quantity or sequence.
- Review cadence (weekly for active jobs; ad hoc when revenue crosses internal threshold).

*Generic pattern; industry-specific compliance is the customer’s responsibility.*
