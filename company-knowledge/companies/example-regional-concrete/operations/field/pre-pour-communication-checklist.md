# Pre-pour communication checklist (example)

**Purpose:** Reduce rework by aligning **office**, **field lead**, and **customer GC** before concrete is on site.  
**Scope:** Example workflow for a growing subcontractor; adapt per contract and locale.

## Before mobilization

- [ ] Latest approved drawings / submittals linked in the job folder (version + date).
- [ ] Pour sequence and truck spacing expectations documented (who owns traffic / pump).
- [ ] Weather contingency agreed (temp/wind; who can delay and how notice is given).

## Day-before

- [ ] Hold point inspections scheduled or waived **in writing** where required.
- [ ] Reinforcement / embeds visually verified against **lift drawings** for **this** pour segment.
- [ ] Emergency contact chain current (field ↔ office ↔ GC).

## Same-day (before truck one)

- [ ] Brief crew on pour plan, edge conditions, and stop-work triggers.
- [ ] Confirm testing plan (slump, cylinders, etc.) matches spec.

## After pour

- [ ] As-built photo or note policy followed; curing protection per spec.

*This file is sample content for the knowledge framework—not legal or engineering advice.*
