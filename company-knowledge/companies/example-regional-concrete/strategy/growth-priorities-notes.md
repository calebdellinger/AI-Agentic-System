# Growth priorities (example narrative)

**Context:** A smaller regional trade contractor aiming to take on **larger commercial packages** without losing quality or cash-flow control.

## Near-term themes

1. **Repeatable handoffs** — Estimating, field, and billing use the same definitions of “done.”
2. **Capacity visibility** — Know crew + equipment + testing bandwidth **before** bid deadlines.
3. **Risk register per job** — Weather, access, long-lead embeds, and inspection bottlenecks listed once and tracked.

## Non-goals (example)

- Winning every bid at low margin to “buy backlog.”
- Expanding geography before core handoffs are reliable.

*Fictional strategy memo for testing retrieval and prompts—not investment advice.*
